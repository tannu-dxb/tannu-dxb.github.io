Projects

Projects Cost & Timeline Analysis - Energy Company

Developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to analyze and monitor procurement data for energy infrastructure projects, encompassing over 700,000 records from azure blob file. The report featured interactive visuals, KPIs, and filters to track project timelines, equipment types, categories (Transmission/Distribution), costs in GBP, and risk levels. Leveraged Power Query for data transformation and DAX for custom measures such as project duration, overdue status, and risk segmentation. Implemented slicers, drill-downs, and advanced navigation using bookmarks and buttons for seamless user experience. The solution provided with actionable insights into budget control, risk mitigation, and project health monitoring, enabling better decision-making and operational efficiency.

IT Service Desk Incident Dashboard - Food Company

Designed and developed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard to monitor and analyze IT service desk incidents, focusing on SLA compliance and operational efficiency. The dashboard provided real-time insights into daily incident volume, severity levels (P1, P2, P3), and assignments by team or individual. Included visual breakdowns of incident trends, status progression (open, in-progress, resolved), and categorized views of Change Requests (CRs), Problem Records (PBIs), and standard incidents. Enabled the service delivery team to identify bottlenecks, improve response times, and ensure SLA adherence through clear KPI indicators, interactive charts, and actionable tables.

Credit Card Application & Usage - Emirates Bank

Designed and delivered an executive Power BI dashboard to track credit card application volumes, approval/rejection rates, and delinquency trends using SQL Server as the backend source. Transformed and modeled data with Power Query and built a semantic layer with calculated columns and DAX measures to derive KPIs such as average spend per customer, fraud risk flags, and delinquency ratios. Performed temporal analysis to identify seasonal sales peaks and high card utilization periods, enabling targeted cashback campaigns. Used trend forecasting and clustering techniques to predict application surges and customer engagement windows, increasing credit card adoption and usage. Integrated drill-through reports and dynamic visuals to support stakeholder decision-making.
